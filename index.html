ZEPHYR

About Us

Ever since it was first organized in 2004, Zephyr has been the most awaited event among the students of Thakur College of Engineering and Technology. This technical festival aims at providing students with a platform to enhance their technical skills. Each Year with a new edition, Zephyr brings new events such as workshops on in-demand technology and skills along with gaming tournaments and fun events. The theme of Zephyr'24 is Spectrum of Innovation. The time traveler, equipped with knowledge about the time period they wish to visit, steps into the machine. The interior is filled with an array of flashing lights, complex instruments, and a central control panel. After entering the desired date and time into the control panel, the traveler activates the machine. As the time machine hums to life, the world outside begins to blur and distort. The traveler witnesses a mesmerizing display of swirling colors and shifting landscapes through the machine's transparent walls. It's as though reality itself is undergoing a profound transformation. This 3-day fest has been recording an increasing footfall of students, teachers and public in general. Join in Zephyr 2024 to get a glimpse of the best of TCET.
